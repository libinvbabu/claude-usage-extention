Privacy Policy

Claude Usage Pace — Last updated: June 2026

Local-only — no data leaves your browser

What this extension does

Claude Usage Pace adds a pacing dashboard to Claude's Usage settings page. It reads the usage percentages and reset labels already visible on that page, calculates pacing metrics, and displays them inline. Everything happens locally in your browser.

Data collected

The extension reads and stores only:

  • Usage percentages and reset labels visible on claude.ai/settings/usage
  • Your preferences: working days, working hours, display options
  • Compact, timestamped snapshots of those percentages (up to 500, stored locally)

No Claude prompts, responses, conversation content, account identifiers, or personal information are ever read or stored.

Where data is stored

All data is stored in chrome.storage.local — on your device only. Nothing is transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

Data sharing

  • No data is sold.
  • No data is transferred to third parties.
  • No data is used for advertising or analytics.
  • No data leaves your browser.

Permissions used

  • storage — saves your preferences and local usage snapshots on this device.
  • Host access to https://claude.ai/* — injects the pacing dashboard and reads visible usage text from Claude's settings page.

No other permissions are requested. The extension does not use tabs, scripting, broad host patterns, or remote code.

Third-party services

None. The extension makes no external network requests of any kind.

Data deletion

Open the extension Options page and click Reset local history to delete all stored snapshots. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data from your browser.

Children's privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If the privacy practices change in a future version, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date will reflect the change.
